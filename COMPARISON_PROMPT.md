Paste this before each new comparison, fill in the brackets, and attach the files.

---

New quote comparison — follow the method in CLAUDE.md.

CLIENT: [name] — [what they do, e.g. tanker hauler / bus operator / general fleet]
RM: [name] ([he/she/they])
FILES: current schedule = [filename]; proposals = [filename(s) + insurer]

Do this:
1. Extract every schedule. Digital = read the text; iPhone/CamScanner photo = read the pages as images. Never guess a figure.
2. Reconcile ALL figures to the cent. Compare on the ALL-IN monthly (VAT + fees + SASRIA included) — flag it if a schedule's headline "total" is before fees/SASRIA/VAT.
3. Line-for-line, current vs each proposal, with a per-line verdict + plain-English why.
4. Find and LEAD with the decisive cover for THIS client type (tanker→pollution; bus→passenger liability; fleet→third-party limit/excess). Credit a proposal for extra cover the current lacks — don't treat added cover as just "more expensive."
5. Recommendation: dearer-but-better still moves; only a genuine cover GAP justifies holding.

First, give me the reconciled line-for-line in chat and confirm every total balances. THEN build:
- an internal PDF pack (findings + questions to the underwriter), and
- a short Afrikaans WhatsApp (informal, mix in English) for the RM.

Be very accurate. Keep it tight on tokens — read only what you need.
